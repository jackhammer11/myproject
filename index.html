Data Science and AI

What is DS?

Data Science is an interdisciplinary field that uses scientific methods, algorithms, processes, and systems to extract knowledge and insights from structured and unstructured data.

What is Artificial Intelligence (AI)?

Artificial Intelligence refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think and act like humans, including learning, problem-solving, and decision-making.

Applications of Data Science and AI

  • Machine Learning
  • Data Mining
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Computer Vision
  • Deep Learning
  • Recommendation Systems

Why Data Science and AI Matter?

Data Science and AI have the potential to revolutionize industries by enabling better decision-making, automation of repetitive tasks, improving efficiency, and driving innovation.
